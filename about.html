Welcome to The Design Report, an interactive data visualization created to help designers understand what the design industry looks like in 2017.

How do designers with different specialities compare across agencies, in-house work, and freelance? How does amount of experience or specific roles affect salaries or stability?

Upon entering the site, simply click on a design specialty that you're interested in to learn more about designers who are currently working in that field. Click on two to compare details in more depth.
